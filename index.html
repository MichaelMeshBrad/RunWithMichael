Michael Holroyd Runs the London Marathon for Autistica

In April 2026, I’ll be running the London Marathon in support of Autistica – the UK’s leading autism and neurodiversity research charity.

I’m autistic and have ADHD, and I’m also a proud dad to two brilliant neurodivergent kids – my daughter is autistic and my son has ADHD. This cause is personal, and every mile I run will be for them – and for everyone who sees the world a little differently.
