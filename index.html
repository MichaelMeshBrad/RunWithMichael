Why I’m Running

My daughter is autistic. My son has ADHD. And I’m proudly neurodivergent myself. I’m running for Autistica because I believe in a world that understands and celebrates difference.

Autistica funds research that improves the lives of autistic people through science – from mental health to employment, communication, and more.
